Pricing & Payments

Money Stuff, Made Clear

Pricing is based on three factors: word count, academic level (undergraduate, masters, PhD), and deadline. Use our price calculator for a live estimate, or send the brief on WhatsApp for a final quote within minutes.

Quotes are always free. You only pay once you've reviewed the price and confirmed the order. We accept bank transfer, card, and most major payment methods. For larger orders, split payments are possible — ask on WhatsApp.

None. The quoted price is final and includes referencing, formatting, a plagiarism check, and unlimited free revisions within the original scope. If the brief shifts significantly mid-project, we'll quote any extra work transparently before continuing.

Yes. First-time students get 10% off, and returning customers receive ongoing loyalty pricing. Bulk orders (multiple assignments, full dissertation chapters) are also discounted — message us for a tailored quote.

Process & Delivery

How the Work Gets Done

We accept urgent briefs as short as 6 hours for shorter pieces (essays, summaries), subject to capacity. Longer or specialised assignments — dissertations, technical reports — need more notice. Send the brief on WhatsApp for an honest answer about feasibility.

Every brief is reviewed manually and matched to a tutor with relevant postgraduate qualifications in your discipline — finance to a finance specialist, law to a law specialist, and so on. We don't use generalists.

Yes. After confirmation, you'll have a direct WhatsApp or email channel to your assigned tutor for clarifications, drafts, and progress updates. No middlemen, no relayed messages.

Free revisions are unlimited within the original scope of the brief. If your tutor missed something, misunderstood a requirement, or the brief evolves slightly mid-project, we rework it at no extra cost. Major changes — adding chapters, switching topics — are quoted separately and agreed first.

Tell us what's not working and we'll fix it. Genuine problems with quality, missed requirements, or misalignment with the brief are addressed through revisions or, in rare cases, a partial refund. Our refund policy is published and applied honestly.
