Our Story

Started by Tutors, Built for Students

Penn Writings began the way most genuinely useful things do — solving a problem we kept seeing. Students arriving at university with strong ideas but no scaffolding for how to express them academically. Researchers stuck in their literature review. International students wrestling with a referencing style nobody had ever properly taught them.

We started informally — a handful of subject specialists, a shared standard for quality, and a simple promise: every student gets work that's tailored to their brief, their level, and their voice. No templates. No recycled essays. No outsourcing.

Today we support thousands of students across the UK, Australia, the US, and beyond. The promise hasn't changed.

What Makes Penn Writings Different

Academic Integrity

How We Think About Responsible Use

We're a tutoring and learning-support service. The work we deliver — model answers, edited drafts, research summaries, sample dissertations — is intended for reference, study, and skill development.

Different universities have different policies on what counts as acceptable use of external support. We trust our students to know their institution's rules and to use our materials in line with them. Our role is to help you understand, learn, and improve — not to replace your engagement with the work.

If you're ever unsure whether a particular type of help is appropriate for your assignment, ask us. We'll tell you honestly.
